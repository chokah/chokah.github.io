Chokah - Bio

Chokah belongs to a generation that witnessed legendary brands rise to greatness, only to see them fade. From Britney's high-profile moments to the decline of MTV and Viva, this was a time of both aspiration and disillusionment. "We were sold a dream," Chokah reflects. "But so much of what was glorified back then crumbled, revealing itself as either unsustainable or simply not what we thought it was. We are the CD generation."

What people today call '90s nostalgia' was just life to us—a life filled with big dreams, empathy, and perhaps a little too much caution. We aimed high, but we also learned to see through the cracks of that glittering facade.

And while technology has evolved, Chokah remains committed to one core principle: "Maybe AI will refine this text, but at the end of the day, only one thing really matters—I demand authenticity and truth in my lyrics."

Oh, and one more thing—Chokah's lyrics? They're all in German.
